# Classroom Web Apps

A collection of interactive, browser-based tools for teaching — primarily A-Level English Language (AQA 7702) but with apps useful across subjects. Everything runs in the browser: no installation, no login, no data leaving the device.

**Browse and launch all apps: [index.html](https://paulfdavies.github.io/index.html)**

---

## What's here

**Language & Linguistics** — interactive exercises covering phonology, grammar, spoken language, cohesion, language change, and diversity. Matching games, drag-and-drop activities, flashcard randomisers, and classroom quizzes, mostly keyed to AQA spec content.

**Exam & Revision Planning** — timetable tools for building personalised revision plans around real exam schedules, with drag-and-drop session management and print-to-PDF output.

**Staff Scheduling** — a constraint-solving rota builder for allocating invigilators across a mock exam week, with availability tracking and CSV export.

**General Tools** — cross-subject utilities including a digital card-sort board, discussion prompt decks, financial literacy resources, and an AI policy discussion mapper.

---

## Highlights

**[PaperSlips.htm](https://paulfdavies.github.io/PaperSlips.htm)** — A freeform digital card-sort board. Type or paste any list of words or phrases and they appear as draggable slips; group, label, and rearrange freely. Import and export CSV to save and restore layouts. Deceptively simple and useful across subjects and activities.

**[GenresFlashcards.htm](https://paulfdavies.github.io/GenresFlashcards.htm)** — Sixteen genre names drawn as single cards or pairs from a shuffled deck. A good quick demo of what the simpler tools in this collection look like in practice.

**[PhonemicRevision.html](https://paulfdavies.github.io/PhonemicRevision.html)** — The full Underhill phonemic chart as an interactive jigsaw. Drag phoneme tiles onto the correct positions with instant feedback, optional colour-coding by category, and voicing shading for consonants.

**[ALExamRevPlanner.html](https://paulfdavies.github.io/ALExamRevPlanner.html)** — A full summer exam revision planner covering the 2026 A-Level and vocational schedule. Select your subjects and get an auto-generated revision timetable with drag-and-drop blocks and print-to-PDF output.

**[Scheduler.html](https://paulfdavies.github.io/Scheduler.html)** — A constraint-solving rota builder for allocating invigilators across a mock exam week. Staff availability, quotas, and clash detection handled automatically; manual drag-and-drop for tweaks; CSV and Google Sheets export.

**[deep-time-lewes.html](https://paulfdavies.github.io/deep-time-lewes.html)** — Geological and evolutionary time scaled onto a walk east from Lewes. The formation of the Earth is roughly as far as Sri Lanka; writing was invented around the corner.
